## 🤝 Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the JobPilot ecosystem a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community of engineers and designers.

---

## 🌟 Our Standards

### Constructive Engagement
Examples of behavior that contributes to a high-performance, positive environment:

- **Empathetic Code Reviews:** Providing constructive, actionable feedback without condescension.
- **Graceful Acceptance:** Acknowledging mistakes, apologizing to those affected, and learning from architectural errors.
- **Inclusive Language:** Utilizing terminology that welcomes developers from all cultural backgrounds.
- **Prioritizing the Ecosystem:** Focusing strictly on what is best for the overall JobPilot community and end-users.

### Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of strictly prohibited behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment of any contributor or maintainer.
- Publishing others' private information (doxxing) without explicit, written permission.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional engineering environment.

## ⚖️ Enforcement & Scope

### Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all JobPilot community spaces (GitHub, Discord, Slack) and also applies when an individual is officially representing the JobPilot community in public spaces or conferences.

### Reporting
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior must be reported immediately to the core maintainer at:
**[GitHub Contact Profile](https://github.com/chauhandigvijay1)**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly, strictly, and fairly. The core team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

### Enforcement Tiers
Project maintainers follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining consequences:

1. **Correction:** A private, written warning providing absolute clarity about the nature of the violation.
2. **Warning:** A formal warning carrying consequences for continued behavior.
3. **Temporary Ban:** A temporary, immediate ban from any community interaction, issues, and pull requests.
4. **Permanent Ban:** A permanent, irrevocable ban from the JobPilot ecosystem.
